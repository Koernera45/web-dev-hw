Ravioli Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 package of cheese ravioli
  • 1.5 cups of cheese.

Directions

  1. In large pan cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink
  2. Grease a baking dish
  3. Layer a third of the spaghetti sauce, half the ravioli and beef, and half cup of cheese
  4. Repeat layers
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 45 minutes
